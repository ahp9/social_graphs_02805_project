'''Sevens football''' is a [[seven-a-side football|seven-a-side]] version of [[association football|football]] played on a smaller [[football pitch]].<ref name="ashique espn">{{cite web |url= https://www.espn.in/football/ashique-kuruniyan/story/3962421/runashiquerun-the-kerala-speedster-who-turned-indias-premier-trickster |title= Run, Ashique, run: The Kerala speedster who turned India's premier trickster|access-date= 2020-01-02|work = ESPN|date=2019-10-13}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite web |date=2022-07-07 |title=FIFA discovers the beauty of Sevens Football: What's this format and why is it so popular in Kerala |url=https://www.espn.in/football/india-ind/story/4695847/fifa-discovers-sevens-football-format-why-is-it-so-popular-in-kerala |access-date=2022-07-26 |website=ESPN |language=en}}</ref> Sevens football is predominantly popular in [[Kerala]], [[Malabar Region|Malabar]], and [[South India]].<ref name="Kerala FA">{{cite web |date=2012-11-20 |title=Sevens football season kicks off in Kerala |url=https://www.goal.com/en-in/news/kerala-sevens-football-isl-indian-football/185ukjvafhx4k18kj7nbu7q658 |access-date=2020-01-02}}</ref> The matches in sevens tournaments are often played to fully packed stadiums.<ref name="sudani">{{cite web |date=2019-10-20 |title=How Kerala shrunk football to make it its own |url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/sports/football/indian-small-football-in-sevens-heaven/articleshow/70905729.cms |work=Times of India |access-date=2020-01-02}}</ref><ref name="ashique espn" /> Sevens tournaments have also attracted players from other parts of India,<ref name="vijayan" /> as well as international players.<ref name="bbc african players">{{cite web |date=2018-08-13 |title=The African footballers changing the game in India |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-asia-india-45085295/the-african-footballers-changing-the-game-in-india |work=BBC |access-date=2020-01-02}}</ref> Matches are usually held from November to May. The Sevens Football Association (SFA) organizes around 50 tournaments across South India.<ref name="indian express sevens">{{cite web |url= https://www.newindianexpress.com/cities/kochi/2019/oct/20/sevens-the-lifeline-of-state-football-2050248.html |title= Sevens the lifeline of country football|access-date= 2020-01-02|work = The New Indian Express|date=2019-09-03}}</ref>
