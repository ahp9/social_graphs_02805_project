In sport a '''semi-closed league''' (also referred to as a '''semi-open league''') is a sports league where a group of teams are guaranteed entry into the league every season, while another group of teams have to qualify or stand at risk of relegation following each season. It is considered a hybrid of [[closed league]]s and leagues with [[promotion and relegation]].
