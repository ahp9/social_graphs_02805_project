A '''sporting director''', or '''director of sport''', is an [[senior management|executive management]] position in a [[sports club]]. The role is well known as a [[management]] role for European football clubs, which are sometime also "sports clubs", offering many types of sports. The sporting director is, in many cases, a member of the [[Board of directors#Inside director|executive board]] and therefore an executive director.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Parnell |first1=Daniel |title=A note to CEOs and Boards on the recruitment of a Sporting Director. The Football Collective. |url=https://e-space.mmu.ac.uk/620294/3/A%20note%20to%20CEOs%20and%20Boards%20on%20recruiting%20a%20Sporting%20Director%20%281%29.pdf |access-date=21 May 2022 |website=e-space.mmu.ac.uk |publisher=Manchester Metropolitan University}}</ref> The sporting director is usually directly subordinate to the [[chief executive officer]] or the [[Chair (officer)|chairman]] of the sports organisation. The sporting director is in turn typically the manager of the [[coaching staff]].<ref>{{cite book |last1=Parnell |first1=Daniel |url=https://www.taylorfrancis.com/chapters/edit/10.4324/9781351262804-13/sporting-director-daniel-parnell-ryan-groom-paul-widdop-sara-ward |title=The sporting director: Exploring current practice and challenges within elite football |last2=Groom |first2=Ryan |last3=Widdop |first3=Paul |last4=Ward |first4=Sara |date=2018 |publisher=Taylor & Francis Group |isbn=9781351262804 |chapter=The sporting director |doi=10.4324/9781351262804-13 |access-date=1 April 2023 |s2cid=188141411}}</ref>

== Director of football ==
A '''director of football''' or '''director of soccer''', sometimes also called a sporting director or technical director, is a senior management figure at an [[association football]] club, most commonly in Europe. Often, their key task is managing [[Transfer (association football)|transfers]] of players to and from the team. However, the nature of the position varies, and their role at a particular club may be more specialised.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Kelly |first=Ryan |date=13 September 2019 |title=What is a director of football & how does transfer chief role work? |url=https://www.goal.com/en-us/news/what-is-a-director-of-football--how-does-transfer-chief-role-work/sy0nzby7kl6j1irjue09n4eog |access-date=20 December 2022 |website=[[Goal (website)|Goal]]}}</ref>
