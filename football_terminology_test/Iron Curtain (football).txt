The '''Iron Curtain''' was the defensive line of [[Rangers F.C.]] during the late 1940s and early 1950s.<ref>[http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/sport/football/scotland/article5014963.ece Rangers pay tribute to legendary Ian McColl]{{dead link|date=September 2024|bot=medic}}{{cbignore|bot=medic}}, ''[[The Sunday Times]]'', 26 October 2008.</ref> The normal line-up in the early 1950s consisted of [[goalkeeper (football)|goalkeeper]] [[Bobby Brown (footballer born 1923)|Bobby Brown]], [[defender (football)|full backs]] [[George Young (Scottish footballer)|George Young]] and [[Jock Shaw]], [[defender (football)|centre half]] [[Willie Woodburn]] and [[midfielders|wing halves]] [[Ian McColl]] and [[Sammy Cox]].<ref>[https://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/scotland/1689332.stm Rangers legend Woodburn dies], [[BBC Sport]], 3 December 2001.</ref> These positions refer to the old [[Formation (association football)#WM|3-2-5 formation]], where the three defenders would mark the two opposing wingers and centre forward, while the wing-halves dealt with the opposing inside forwards. These direct match-ups meant that Rangers' Iron Curtain had several great confrontations with [[Hibernian F.C.|Hibs]]' [[The Famous Five (football)|Famous Five]] forward line of [[Gordon Smith (footballer born 1924)|Gordon Smith]], [[Bobby Johnstone]], [[Lawrie Reilly]], [[Eddie Turnbull]] and [[Willie Ormond]].<ref>[http://scotlandonsunday.scotsman.com/football/Ex-Scotland-manager-McColl-dies.4630488.jp Ex Scotland manager McColl dies, aged 81] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110609040131/http://scotlandonsunday.scotsman.com/football/Ex-Scotland-manager-McColl-dies.4630488.jp |date=9 June 2011 }}, ''[[The Scotsman]]'', 26 October 2008.</ref>

The lineup in the mid-1940s of [[Jerry Dawson (Scottish footballer)|Jerry Dawson]], [[Dougie Gray]], Shaw, [[Scot Symon]], Young and Cox was also commonly referred to as the Iron Curtain.<ref name = "young obit">[[Tam Dalyell|Dalyell, Tam]]. [http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_qn4158/is_19970113/ai_n9642674/ Obituary: George Young], ''[[The Independent]]'', 13 January 1997.</ref> This was done to the extent that most young people in Scotland would associate the term with Rangers, rather than the [[Iron Curtain|geopolitical situation]].<ref name = "young obit"/>
