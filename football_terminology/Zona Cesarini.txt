'''Zona Cesarini''' ({{langx|en|Cesarini zone}}) is an Italian expression used when talking about events or situations that happened, are happening or will happen at the last minute and when something is at its final stages.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ilpost.it/2018/12/22/zona-cesarini/|title=Perché si dice "zona Cesarini"|date=22 December 2018 |publisher=ilpost.it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lavocedimanduria.it/articolo/che-cose-la-zona-cesarini-il-significato-e-lorigine-della-celebre-espressione_59953|title=Che cos'è la zona Cesarini? Il significato e l'origine della celebre espressione|publisher=lavocedimanduria.it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.gonews.it/2022/08/11/che-cose-la-zona-cesarini-e-perche-si-chiama-cosi/|title=Che cos'è la "zona Cesarini" e perché si chiama così?|date=11 August 2022 |publisher=gonews.it}}</ref>

The expression is named after Italian footballer [[Renato Cesarini]], who was known for scoring goals during the last few minutes of games.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/es/noticias/la-zona-cesarini-o-por-que-nunca-hay-que-rendirse/1cppplwqr90oa1edbaqoc3hbgt|title=La Zona Cesarini o por qué nunca hay que rendirse|publisher=goal.com (Archived)|access-date=2023-09-12|archive-date=2023-04-19|archive-url=https://archive.today/20230419180458/https://www.goal.com/es/noticias/la-zona-cesarini-o-por-que-nunca-hay-que-rendirse/1cppplwqr90oa1edbaqoc3hbgt|url-status=bot: unknown}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/it/notizie/renato-cesarini-il-ce-larte-del-goal-allo-scadere-e-la-leggenda-della-zona/1e6xnkf2offma1dop7q06vpmz4|title=Perché si chiama Zona Cesarini?|publisher=goal.com (Archived)|access-date=2023-09-12|archive-date=2023-04-25|archive-url=https://archive.today/20230425215049/https://www.goal.com/it/notizie/renato-cesarini-il-ce-larte-del-goal-allo-scadere-e-la-leggenda-della-zona/1e6xnkf2offma1dop7q06vpmz4|url-status=bot: unknown}}</ref>
